Quick Introduction

Programme Highlights

  • 16 hours of live sessions (2 hours each Saturday & Sunday)
  • 50 hours of assignments via curated worksheets
  • 15 days of structured assignments for practical application
  • 6 months of support for handling clients
  • Lifelong membership in our interns community
  • Mentorship, feedback and career guidance
  • Blend of theory and practice: case studies, reflective activities & creative therapies
