Modules Overview

  • Module 1: Role of Counsellors – understand the various responsibilities and environments in which counsellors work.
  • Module 2: Values & Ethics – learn about professional standards, empathy, respect and confidentiality; explore ethical dilemmas through role‑play.
  • Module 3: Challenges for Beginner Counsellors – discuss common challenges, boundary management, self‑care and stress management strategies.
  • Module 4: Active Listening & Observing Client Behaviour – practise paraphrasing, reflecting and analysing non‑verbal cues.
  • Module 5: Rapport Building – master techniques for establishing trust, empathy and a therapeutic alliance.
  • Module 6: Creative Therapies – explore art, music, movement and somatic therapies for holistic healing.
  • Module 7: Integration of Spirituality & Psychotherapy – learn how to incorporate mindfulness, meditation and spiritual considerations ethically.
  • Module 8: Supervision & Reflective Practice – develop self‑reflection skills and understand the importance of ongoing supervision.
  • Module 9: Professional Development & Career Planning – build your professional identity, portfolio and network.
  • Module 10: Evaluation & Closure – assess learning outcomes, receive feedback and plan the next steps in your career.
